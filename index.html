The Challenge

Decoding the world's largest biometric database.

The Aadhaar ecosystem generates massive volumes of enrolment and update logs. While operationally efficient, this data holds untapped potential for understanding societal shifts.

Our goal is to analyze anonymized datasets to detect **migration corridors**, **demographic gaps**, and **service delivery anomalies** without compromising user privacy.
